## Froth on Top (Lunch/Dinner)
1. Mashed potatoes (not on the menu, but just ask them; it's insanely tasty. I can't explain it, they've just managed to work miracles with the darn thing)
2. Chicken nuggets with tartar sauce
3. Mutton kheema rice - Good, not great though

## Pubbas: (Dessert) (In order of my preference)
1. Chocolate Dad
2. Tiramisu
3. Gudbud
4. Pizza chocolate
5. Parfait

## BonBons (Take home cakes)
1. Ice Cream cake
2. Day and Night
3. Rich Chocolate cake with nuts
4. Chocolate Deloitte
5. Butterscotch cake

## Maharajas (Lunch)
1. Anjal Fish masala Fry
2. Banguda Rava Fry
3. Pomfret tawa fry
4. Cheese Dal makhni
5. Neer dosa
6. Chicken sukka
7. Ghee Roast chicken

## GiriManjas
1. Any fish with hand pound rice

## Pop Tates (Dinner)
1. Grilled chicken

## Juice Romantic (Evening)
1. Chocolate Sharjah drink

## Hao Ming (Lunch/Dinner)
1. Shin Min Rice/Noodles - Chicken + Pork (Goddamit, this is drugs) - Make sure you have it at the restaurant. Don't do takeaways.

## Bombay Lucky
1. Bheja Fry
2. Bheja masala

## Pereira's
1. Beef biryani
2. Pork fry, pork chilly
3. Beef Ghee Roast

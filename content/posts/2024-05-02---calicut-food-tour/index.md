# Calicut Food Tour

### Best Biriyani (very personal opinion)

*   Beef Biriyani from Kuttichira Biriyani Centre (KBC)
*   Chicken Leghorn Biriyani from Paragon
*   Beef Biriyani from Rahmath Hotel
*   Chicken Biriyani from Kuttichira Biriyani Centre (KBC)

### Other Main Course

*   The usual suspects - Paragon, Topform, Sagar, M-Grill, Bombay Hotel (all time top restaurants in Kozhikode)
*   Signature dishes from Shaap Restaurant at South Beach - overall great food
*   Grillax at South Beach - Veal Chops, Grilled chicken etc
*   Samudra Sadya from Ambika Hotel (only on Sundays)
*   Vada + Dosa from Sri Venkatesh Lunch Home near Corporation office
*   'Edayile Kada' at South Beach - Upmah & Beef for Breakfast, Pazhampori & Beef
*   Champion Hotel - Porotta, Fish Fry, Beef Chilli
*   Mutton Mandhi from White House (worth the travel, expensive though)
*   Beef Dry Fry from Hotel Sarovar
*   Porotta / Puttu and Beef from Mammali's Kovoor
*   Veg food from Gulmohar, Eranhipalam - Go only in the evenings though. Breakfast ain't great there. Paneer items etc were pretty good.
*   🦐 Prawns Thava Fry from 1980 : A Nostalgic Restaurant at Beach. Moru is also nice. (Service is shitty)
*   Jafferkka Soya Bean Fry at Halwa Bazaar
*   Beef Dry Fry / Chicken Fry from Old Sagar
*   Fish curry meals from Amma mess
*   Katsu - Pan Asian cuisine
*   Rabbit Meat + Paal Kappa at Paropadi Toddy shop
*   Sreedharettante Puttu Kada
*   Arabian Dishes from Zaitoon

### Snacks

*   Top pick : Mushakkals cafe - Nadakkavu
*   Ari Kadukka - Kallummakkaya with rice filling from Thattukada in front of Crown Theatre
*   Zains hotel - Thalassery Snacks
*   Chaaya Cafe - Malabari Snacks
*   Aadaminte Chaayakkada - Malabari Snacks
*   Shawarma from Al Taza
*   Kozhi Ada (Beef Snack) from Manda Store
*   Savoury Sea Shell Parayanchery

### Shakes & Desserts

*   Abooda and Saboora shake from Suresh Veg & Fruits Malaparambu
*   Sharjah Shake from EPK Kalanthans - The birthplace of Sharjah Shake.
*   Paal Sarvath from Bhaskarettan's Shop near Paragon
*   Elaneer Shake at Kuttichira
*   Kunafa Hut near Corporation office
*   Halwa & Chips from Kumari Chips (Opp to Paragon) or Sankaran Bakery (SM Street)
*   The Bakers and Co Cafe
*   Falooda from Sangeeth Cool Bar

### Cafes to Chill

*   Kadalas cafe at Beach - phenomenal insane view, food is average
*   Brown Town at Pottammal
*   Club Sulaimani - Great view - Dynamite Squid is really good, you get Bubble tea too
*   Cafficana near ULCC
*   Lot of very nice cafés on PT Usha road
*   Lady Loafella - Epic food - Beef Goulash and Chimichurri Rice, Chicken Croissant Sandwich, Coconut Cucumber (Drink), Molten Chocolate Cake with Berry Compote and Vanilla Ice Cream (Dessert)
*   Eatmosphere - Shitty food but the terrace seating is a nice vibe

### Continental Food

*   Groza, PT Usha Road (closed now?)
*   BeyondBurg Inc, PT Usha Road
*   Lady Loafella
*   Beef Steak from Latin Street

### Arabic Food

*   The Light House Restaurant (Arabian food)
*   Zatar near Beach

### Fine Dining

*   Raviz Kadavu
*   KPM Tripenta - Food is decent
*   Taj Gateway
*   Apollo Dimora - very average food
*   Copper Folia
